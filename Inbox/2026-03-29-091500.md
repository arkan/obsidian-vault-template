# Interesting article on SaaS pricing

Read an article on value-based pricing for B2B SaaS. Key points:
- Don't price by number of features but by delivered value
- Usage metrics > number of seats
- Offer a generous free plan for acquisition

Relevant for the [[SaaS-Dashboard]] pricing strategy.
